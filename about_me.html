Calista Dance

Artistic Statement

Without a solid foundation in technique, it is impossible for a dancer to ever improve. I believe that this technique mostly comes from ballet. Ballet provides the strength, elegance, and grace for a dancer to build upon when beginning to explore other styles.

Experience and Certifications

  • Rehearsing and performing with young children
  • 4 years experience in pre-professional ballet company
  • In progress Bachelors of Dance Education at Grand Canyon University
  • Cecchetti Ballet Teaching Certificate 1
